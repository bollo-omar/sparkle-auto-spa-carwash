About

This project draws inspiration from a car wash busines which offers employemnet to jobless youths. We noticed they also needed an online presense to market their services and keep track of their clients and revenues.

For now we are not integrating an payment gateway, but will keep records of cash payments and mobile money(MPesa)

This projct should take approximately one month for it to be complete(Web) and secon iteration should should come with a mobile app
